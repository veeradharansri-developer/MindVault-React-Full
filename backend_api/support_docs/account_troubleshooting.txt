MindVault Account Troubleshooting

Guidelines for resolving login errors, resetting passwords, and updating account metadata.

1. Resetting Your Password:
   - Click the "Forgot Password" link on the MindVault login page.
   - Enter your registered email address to receive a secure reset link.
   - The password reset link expires after twenty-four (24) hours.

2. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) Lockouts:
   - If you lose access to your authenticator app, please retrieve your recovery code saved during initial MFA setup.
   - If recovery codes are unavailable, email security@mindvault.ai for identity verification and manual reset.

Contact: support@mindvault.ai
