MindVault Shipping & Delivery FAQ

This document outlines our shipping timelines, courier options, and delivery rates for hardware tokens and physical merchandise.

1. Timelines & Shipping Rates:
   - Domestic shipping (US & Canada): takes 3-5 business days. Free for orders above $100. Standard rate of $9.99 applies otherwise.
   - International shipping: takes 7-14 business days depending on customs clearance. Standard rate of $24.99 applies.

2. Tracking Orders:
   - Once shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email from our logistics partner.
   - Tracking statuses update within 24 hours of package handoff.
