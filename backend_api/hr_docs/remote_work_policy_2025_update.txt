MindVault AI Remote Work Policy Update (2025)

Effective January 1, 2025, this update introduces specific rules for new hires and overrides the remote work allowances described in the 2023 policy.

Key Rules for New Hires:
1. Probation Period: All new hires are required to work from their designated physical office 5 days a week (full-time in-office) during their initial six (6) month probation period. Remote work is not permitted during this time.
2. Post-Probation: Upon successful completion of the probation period, new hires may transition to a hybrid model. However, they are permitted to work remotely for a maximum of two (2) days per week, rather than the three (3) days outlined in the 2023 policy.
3. This policy update applies to all employees starting on or after January 1, 2025. Pre-existing employees hired before this date are grandfathered under the 2023 policy.

Approved by: Priya Sharma, Head of HR Operations
