MindVault AI General Remote Work Policy (2023 Edition)

This policy outlines the guidelines for remote work at MindVault AI, established in January 2023.
Under this policy, all full-time employees are eligible for hybrid work. Employees can work remotely for up to three (3) days per week, with the remaining two (2) days spent in their primary office location.

Key Rules:
1. Core Hours: Regardless of location, employees must be available during core business hours from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local time.
2. Approval: Remote work arrangements must be requested and approved in writing by the employee's direct department head.
3. Equipment: The company provides a standard laptop, keyboard, and mouse. Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe, ergonomic, and quiet workspace with high-speed internet.
4. Security: Employees must use the company VPN and adhere to all data privacy guidelines when working outside the office.

Contact: HR Operations Team at hr@mindvault.ai
