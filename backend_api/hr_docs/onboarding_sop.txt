MindVault AI Onboarding Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

This document details the step-by-step onboarding process for all new employees joining MindVault AI.

Step 1: Pre-onboarding (1 week before joining)
- Collect signed offer letter and basic employee information.
- IT Department: Provision laptop, create corporate email address, and setup software licenses.
- HR: Prepare the welcoming packet.

Step 2: Day 1 (Welcome & IT Setup)
- 9:30 AM: Welcome session with the Onboarding Lead.
- 11:00 AM: IT equipment handoff and setup. Ensure VPN, email, and slack are working.
- 2:00 PM: Introduction to the designated 'Onboarding Buddy' from the team.
- 3:00 PM: General HR paperwork completion.

Step 3: Week 1 (Orientation & Training)
- Team and project orientations.
- Access requests for repository, database, and dev servers.
- Mandatory training modules (Security, Privacy, and Code of Conduct).

Step 4: Month 1 (First checkpoint)
- Mid-month check-in by HR.
- 30-day performance expectations review with direct manager.

Owned by: Arjun Mehta, HR Onboarding Lead
