DOCUMENT TYPE: Internal Policy
TITLE: Data Retention & Customer Data Handling Policy
OWNER: Priya Nandan, Head of Compliance
LAST UPDATED: March 12, 2025
STATUS: Active

1. PURPOSE
This policy defines how customer data collected through our platform is stored,
archived, and eventually deleted, in compliance with regional data protection
requirements.

2. RETENTION PERIODS
- Active customer accounts: data retained indefinitely while account is active.
- Closed/churned accounts: data retained for 24 months, then anonymized.
- Support tickets and chat logs: retained for 12 months.
- Billing and invoice records: retained for 7 years (legal requirement).

3. BACKGROUND
This policy was introduced after the Q3 2024 customer data audit (see
"Project Atlas Postmortem") revealed inconsistent deletion practices across
teams. Prior to this policy, some teams were deleting churned customer data
after only 30 days, which caused issues when Finance needed historical billing
records for tax filings.

4. APPROVAL CHAIN
Drafted by Priya Nandan, reviewed by Legal (Ramesh Iyer), approved by CTO
Anjali Kapoor. Any changes to retention periods require sign-off from both
Legal and Compliance.

5. OPEN QUESTION
Engineering (Karthik Subramanian) has flagged that the 24-month retention
for closed accounts may conflict with upcoming EU-style "right to be
forgotten" requests. This is still under review as of this document's last
update and is not yet resolved.
