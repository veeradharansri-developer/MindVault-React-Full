MindVault AI Leave Policy

We believe in maintaining a healthy work-life balance. This document outlines the leave benefits and regulations at MindVault AI.

1. Sick Leave:
   - Full-time employees are allotted twelve (12) days of sick leave per calendar year.
   - Sick leave is intended for personal medical recovery.
   - For absences exceeding three (3) consecutive business days, a registered medical practitioner's note/certificate must be submitted to HR.

2. Casual Leave:
   - Employees are allotted ten (10) days of casual leave per calendar year.
   - Casual leave is intended for personal matters, vacations, or general time off.
   - All casual leave requests must be submitted in the HR portal and approved by the direct manager at least 3 business days in advance.

3. Parental Leave:
   - Paid Maternity Leave: MindVault AI provides sixteen (16) weeks of fully paid maternity leave to eligible biological mothers.
   - Paid Paternity Leave: We provide four (4) weeks of fully paid paternity leave to eligible partners/fathers.
   - Leave can be taken continuously or in agreed chunks within the first year of the child's birth or adoption.

Contact: HR Leaves Team at leaves@mindvault.ai
