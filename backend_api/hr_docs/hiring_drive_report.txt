Campus Hiring Drive Report (September 2025)

Executive Summary:
MindVault AI conducted a campus recruitment drive in September 2025 across five major engineering institutions to hire Graduate Engineer Trainees (GETs).

Results:
- Total candidates screened: 450
- Offers extended: 22
- Offers accepted: 20 (joining date set for July 2026)

Key Learnings and Observations:
1. Technical Tests: Many candidates and interviewers noted that the initial coding and technical tests were excessively long, leading to candidate fatigue. We should compress the online tests to 60 minutes.
2. Speed Interviewing: The speed interviewing rounds (15-minute quick evaluations for cultural fit) were a massive success. They allowed us to interview more candidates and get high-quality feedback quickly.
3. Competitiveness: Top-tier graduates received competing offers. To secure high acceptance rates next year, we need to finalize offers faster and consider signing bonuses.
