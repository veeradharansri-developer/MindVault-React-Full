HR Policy Meeting Notes - October 15, 2024

Attendees:
- Priya Sharma (Head of HR Operations)
- Arjun Mehta (HR Onboarding Lead)
- Sarah Jenkins (Engineering Representative)

Agenda:
Update the remote work policy for new hires to enhance collaboration and onboarding quality.

Discussion:
Arjun Mehta pointed out that new engineering hires who started working fully remotely from Day 1 struggled to integrate with their team, leading to longer ramp-up times and lower engagement. Priya Sharma agreed that physical presence during the onboarding phase is crucial for team alignment and corporate culture integration.

Decisions & Action Items:
- All new hires starting from January 1, 2025 will be required to work from the office 5 days a week for their first six months (probation period).
- After probation, they can work hybrid, up to 2 days remote per week (instead of 3 days under the 2023 general policy).
- Priya Sharma will formally approve the policy document.
- Arjun Mehta will update the onboarding SOP to reflect these requirements.

Approved by: Priya Sharma, Head of HR Operations
